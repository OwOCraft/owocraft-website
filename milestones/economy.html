Economy Milestone

The economy requires a bank, one made of light colors (white etc). it also needs to be 20x20 blocks and can be at any height. The bank also needs to be placed in OwOCity. The Bank must contain a detailed interior that is usable* and looks nice.

* = things put in hoppers/chests can be stored underground
