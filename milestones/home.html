/home Commands

The /home commands allow you to teleport to your home. Teleportation to homes requires the Economy Milestone and a Hotel that can fit every Player in OwOCraft. The Hotel must have at least 20 Rooms, all of which have a bed, storage, lighting, Crafting Tables and furnaces.
