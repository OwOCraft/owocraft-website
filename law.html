CATEGORY 1. IN SERVER LAWS

Section 1. Murder

If someone on OwOCraft murders another person without consent, the time in prison is 1 day and/or can do 2 days of Community Service. (If no court case happens, the fine can be up to $250)

Section 1a. Self Defence

If someone is attacking and you accidentally kill them back, the sentence can be dropped entirely unless if the offender accidentally hits you. (If no court case happens, the fine can be up to $250)

Section 2. Destruction of Property

If someone on OwOCraft is destroying someone else’s property, they can serve 1 – 10 days in prison and/or 2 – 20 days Community Service. The time served is determined by how much of the property was destroyed.

Section 3. Trespassing

Trespassing on OwOCraft is not allowed. If you are trespassing and you do not leave after first request, you can be imprisoned for 1 day and/or 2 days Community Service. (If no court case happens, the fine can be up to $150)

Section 4. Hate Speech

Bullying and the harassment of others is not tolerated. OwOCraft does not tolerate any homophobic, transphobic, sexist or racist speech. Punishment for Hate Speech is 1 – 72 days imprisonment, up to 128 days Community Service and/or up to 70 days server ban. 

Section 5. OwOCraft Citizen ID

To become a citizen of OwOCraft, you must register for an OwOCraft Citizen ID. If you played OwOCraft before OwOCraft 12, this doesn’t apply. To apply for an ID, visit owocraft.logzinga.com/ocid.html.

Section 6. Duplication

If you duplicate items on OwOCraft, you can be imprisoned for upto 15 days, upto 10 days Community Service, removal of duped items, and/or a server ban.

Section 7. Court/Imprisonment

If someone needs to go to court or prison, they must be killed beforehand, teleportation is not allowed.

Section 8. Thievery 

Stealing on OwOCraft is illegal and always will be. Punishment for stealing is upto 2 days Prison and/or upto 4 days Community Service.


CATEGORY 2. WAR RELATED EVENTS

Section 1. Applied laws

The only applied law is Section 4.

Section 2. Starting War

War must be started with at least two consenting parties. All rules for the war must also be written inside the declaration of war, which must be signed by a member of OwOCraft Government.

CATEGORY 3. SERVER PLUGIN/WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT

Section 1. Requirements

You require a GitHub account, some base knowledge of either Java or HTML and you must be added to the OwOCraft Organisation.

Section 2. Communication

You must have good communication with the rest of the OwOCraft Developers.

Section 3. Commit Messages

Every commit message must say what changes you have done to a specific file.

Section 4. Punishments

If you destroy any repositories containing any code, you will be removed from the OwOCraft Group and banned from OwOCraft for up to 30 Days.


CATEGORY 4. GENERAL RULES

Section 1. Loopholes

If you find a loophole, contact logzinga, reporting loopholes can give you more items in game. If you abuse a loophole however, you can go to Prison for upto 5 days.

Section 2. Administrator Abuse

If any members of OwOCraft Government is abusing their power, contact another member of OwOCraft Government or start a court case.
